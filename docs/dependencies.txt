You will need at least these:
 - A compiler. The makefiles are written for GCC
 - SDL
 - SDL_Mixer
 - SDL_Image
 - zziplib
 - libconfuse
 - gl
 - glu
 - flex
 
For compiling on a recent Ubuntu:
 - g++
 - make
 - libsdl1.2-dev
 - libsdl-image1.2-dev
 - libsdl-mixer1.2-dev
 - libzzip-dev
 - libconfuse-dev
 - libgl1-mesa-dev
 - libglu1-mesa-dev
 - flex
