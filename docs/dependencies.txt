You will need at least these:
 - A compiler. The makefiles are written for GCC
 - SDL 1.2
 - SDL_Mixer 1.2
 - SDL_Image 1.2
 - SDL_Net 1.2
 - zziplib
 - libconfuse
 - gl
 - glu
 - glew
 - Lua
 - Freetype2
 - Bullet Physics
 
For compiling on a recent Ubuntu:
 - g++
 - make
 - flex
   
 - libsdl1.2-dev
 - libsdl-image1.2-dev
 - libsdl-mixer1.2-dev
 - libsdl-net1.2-dev
 - libzzip-dev
 - libconfuse-dev
 - libgl1-mesa-dev
 - libglu1-mesa-dev
 - libglew-dev
 - liblua5.1-0-dev
 - libfreetype6-dev
 
 - Compile bullet from lib-external
