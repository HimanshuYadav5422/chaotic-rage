Each unitclass has a melee weapon and two starting weapons.

The two starting weapons can be swapped with others,
picked up and dropped. On death, all current weapons are dropped.
There is a max of 2 weapons at a time.

The player needs to choose their current weapon using an interfce of some description.

Each unitclass also has a special ability which can be used at any time.

Some generic weapons:
 - Sheep gun
 - Minigun
 - Moatar - is a two player weapon, one carries weapon, the other carries ammo
 - Energy sword
